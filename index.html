About This Service

I'm a fellow reader who created this service out of a personal need — I love listening to books while commuting, exercising, or just relaxing, and I wanted my audiobooks available on all my devices without restrictions.

What started as a personal project turned into this: helping other readers who want the same flexibility. I use high-quality AI voices and manually record each file to ensure clean, natural-sounding MP3s.

Since I'm just one person who also reads for fun and learning, orders may take a few days. I appreciate your understanding and support! 🙌
